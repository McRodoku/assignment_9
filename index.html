How to make a Thanksgiving Turkey

 

To start, you'll need these ingredients:

  • 1 turkey
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) of unsalted butter, softened
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil or cooking spray

  • Optional things:
    • Seasonings (such as thyme, rosemary, sage, etc)
    • Stuffing
    • Cooking twine (for trussing)

    You'll also need this equipment:

  • Roasting pan with a rack
  • Meat thermometer
  • Aluminum foil
  • Basting brush or turkey baster

    • Instructions:

    Thaw the turkey

    If your turkey is frozen, ensure it's fully thawed before cooking. This can take several days in the refrigerator, so plan accordingly. Follow the turkey's packaging for specific thawing guidelines.
