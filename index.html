How to make a Thanksgiving Turkey

 

To start, you'll need these ingredients:

  • 1 turkey
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) of unsalted butter, softened
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking oil or cooking spray

  • Optional things:
    • Seasonings (such as thyme, rosemary, sage, etc)
    • Stuffing
    • Cooking twine (for trussing)

    You'll also need this equipment:

  • Roasting pan with a rack
  • Meat thermometer
  • Aluminum foil
  • Paper towels
  • Basting brush or turkey baster

    • Instructions:

    Thaw the turkey
  • If your turkey is frozen, ensure it's fully thawed before cooking. This can take several days in the
    refrigerator, so plan accordingly. Follow the turkey's packaging for specific thawing guidelines.

    • Preheat the oven
  • Preheat your oven to 325°F (or 163°C for you Non-Americans that happen to be celebrating
    Thanksgiving for whatever reason).
    • Prepare the turkey
  • Remove the turkey from its packaging and remove any giblets and the neck from the cavities.
    You can use these for making gravy if you like.
  • Rinse the turkey under cold water and pat it dry with paper towels.
  • Season the inside of the turkey with salt, pepper, and optional seasonings.
    You can also stuff the turkey with your favorite stuffing if desired.
